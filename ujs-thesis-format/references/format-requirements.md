# 江苏大学毕业设计（论文）写作规范 2026 要点

Source: `../assets/jiangsu-university-thesis-writing-standard-2026.pdf`.

Use this reference for Jiangsu University undergraduate graduation design/thesis documents written mainly in Chinese. The uploaded PDF says foreign-language thesis writing follows separately issued rules. If an official college-specific template or the PDF's attachment template is later provided, use it for exact cover/style details and keep these rules as the baseline.

## Page And Visual Layout

- Paper: A4 white paper, 210 mm x 297 mm.
- Margins: top 25 mm, left 25 mm, bottom 20 mm, right 20 mm.
- Binding: left-side binding.
- Source-PDF visual style: left-aligned header text with a horizontal rule; centered Arabic page number in footer.
- Observed fonts in the source PDF: FZXiaoBiaoSong-B05S, FangSong, SimHei, KaiTi, Times New Roman.
- Font color: default all text to black (`#000000`) unless the user or an official college template explicitly requires another color. This includes headings, body text, captions, tables, references, headers, and footers.
- Recommended fallback when no official Word template is available:
  - Body Chinese: FangSong or an available FangSong-compatible CJK font.
  - Headings: SimHei or an available Hei-style CJK font.
  - Latin/numbers: Times New Roman.
  - Use first-line indentation for body paragraphs and consistent line spacing.

## Required Document Order

Use this order unless the user or college template says otherwise:

1. 封面
2. 原创性声明
3. 序或前言（可选）
4. 中文摘要（含关键词）
5. 英文摘要（含关键词）
6. 目录
7. 插图和附表清单（可选）
8. 注释表（可选）：含符号、标志、缩略词、首字母缩写、单位、术语、名词等
9. 引言（可选）
10. 正文
11. 结论或结语
12. 参考文献
13. 致谢（可选）
14. 附录（可选）：附录 A、附录 B、附录 C

## Cover

- Include thesis title, college name, major/class, student name, supervisor name, supervisor professional title, and date.
- Title must be concise and accurate.
- Keep title within 30 Chinese characters when possible.
- Use a subtitle only when needed to avoid an overly long main title.

## Abstracts And Keywords

- Abstracts must be objective, independent, and self-contained.
- Chinese abstract: at least 400 Chinese characters.
- Foreign-language abstract: correspond to the Chinese abstract and contain at least 350 content words.
- Abstract content should cover purpose/content, method/process, results/conclusions, and major innovations.
- Do not add annotations in abstracts.
- Avoid formulas, chemical structures, charts/tables, uncommon abbreviations, non-public symbols/terms, and reference citation numbers in abstracts.
- Keywords appear below the abstract, starting a new line at the lower left.
- Use 3 to 5 keywords.
- Separate Chinese keywords with `；`.
- Prefer standardized terms, for example from 《汉语主题词表》.

## Table Of Contents

- List document components, chapters, and section names in order.
- Include three heading levels.
- Keep TOC headings consistent with body headings.
- Include conclusion, references, acknowledgements, and appendices in order when present.

## Main Text

- Body should describe the research work in detail: problem, core view, method, data/charts, results, and discussion.
- Typical length: 10,000 to 15,000 Chinese characters/words equivalent; theses with unique insight are not limited by this range.
- Symbols, abbreviations, drawing standards, and measurement units must follow national standards or common disciplinary standards.
- Define author-created symbols, marks, and abbreviations at first use.

## Figures

- Figures include curves, structural drawings, diagrams, explanatory drawings, block diagrams, flowcharts, records, layouts, maps, photos, plates, and similar items.
- Number all figures consecutively with Arabic numerals: `图 1`, `图 2`, ...
- Give every figure a concise and accurate title.
- Place the figure number and title below the figure.

## Tables

- Number all tables consecutively with Arabic numerals: `表 1`, `表 2`, ...
- Give every table a concise and accurate title.
- Place the table number and title above the table.
- Put symbol/mark/code explanations as a table note below the table title or below the table.
- Align numbers vertically within the same column.
- Do not use `同上`, `同左`, `；`, or similar placeholders in tables. Fill in exact values/text.

## Formulas

- Number formulas, expressions, and equations consecutively with Arabic numerals.
- Place the formula number at the far right of the formula line.
- If a formula wraps, put the number on the last line.

## Units

- Use SI units as the basis.
- Follow GB3100 to GB3102-93.
- Non-physical quantity units may combine Chinese characters and symbols, such as `件/台` or `元/km`.

## Heading Numbering

- Heading levels must be unified, orderly, and clear.
- Content under each heading must match that heading.
- Science, engineering, agriculture, medicine, and education normally use hierarchical Arabic numbering:
  - Level 1: `1`, `2`, `3`
  - Level 2: `2.1`, `2.2`, `2.3`
  - Level 3: `2.2.1`, `2.2.2`, `2.2.3`
- Humanities, law, economics, management, and arts normally use:
  - Level 1: `一、`, `二、`, `三、`
  - Level 2: `（一）`, `（二）`, `（三）`
  - Level 3: `1.`, `2.`, `3.`
- Usually do not exceed four heading levels.
- Do not add punctuation at the end of heading numbers.

## Conclusion

- Summarize and synthesize the whole research work.
- Include conclusions and possible ideas/suggestions for further discussion.

## References

- References are required.
- List works the author directly read or cited.
- Prefer authoritative and up-to-date literature.
- Use at least 15 Chinese and foreign references in total.
- Mark in-text citations with bracketed sequence numbers such as `[1]`.
- Each reference entry ends with `.`.
- Follow GB/T 7714-2015.
- Literature type codes include `[M]`, `[C]`, `[G]`, `[N]`, `[J]`, `[D]`, `[R]`, `[S]`, `[P]`, `[DB]`, `[CP]`, `[EB]`, `[A]`, `[CM]`, `[DS]`, `[Z]`.

## Acknowledgements And Appendices

- Acknowledgements are optional and usually placed on a separate page after references.
- Appendices are optional and hold supporting material unsuitable for the main text, such as raw data, mathematical derivations, programs, diagrams, structural drawings, notes, statistical tables, and computer printouts.
